# Code of Conduct

Positron contributors are expected to keep technical collaboration respectful, factual, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Give actionable, evidence-based feedback.
- Respect different backgrounds and experience levels.
- Keep issue and review discussions on scope.
- Protect private data, credentials, and repository information.
- Accept maintainer decisions on safety, release, and scope controls.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information or credentials
- Deliberately bypassing repository safety controls
- Disruptive or bad-faith issue and review activity

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content and restrict participation when necessary.
